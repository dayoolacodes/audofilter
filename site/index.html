AudioFilter

Free Chrome extension that automatically mutes audio when profanity appears in video subtitles. Works on any website.

Features

🎵

Smart Muting

Intercepts subtitle files to pre-schedule mutes with precise timing. Falls back to on-screen subtitle detection.

🌐

Works Everywhere

YouTube, Netflix, Prime Video, Disney+, Hulu, Twitter/X, and any website with video subtitles.

⚙

Customizable

Add or remove blocked words. Adjust mute duration and timing. Your settings sync across devices.

🔒

Private

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data collected, no servers, no tracking. Fully open source.

💰

100% Free

No subscription, no ads, no premium tier. Free forever.

⚡

DRM Safe

Uses tab-level muting instead of touching video elements, so streaming services work without issues.

How It Works

1

Install the extension

Add AudioFilter from the Chrome Web Store. Enable it and you're ready to go.

2

Turn on subtitles

Enable subtitles/captions on any video. AudioFilter reads the subtitle text to detect profanity.

3

Watch clean

When a blocked word appears, the tab audio is automatically muted for the duration of the word.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Data Collection

AudioFilter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What AudioFilter Does

  • Reads on-screen subtitle text to detect blocked words
  • Intercepts subtitle file downloads (TTML/WebVTT) to parse timing data
  • Temporarily mutes the browser tab when a blocked word is detected
  • Stores your settings (blocked word list, mute duration) locally via Chrome's sync storage

Permissions Explained

  • tabs — Required to mute/unmute the browser tab audio
  • storage — Required to save your settings
  • Host permissions — Required to run on streaming sites that play video with subtitles

Third-Party Services

AudioFilter does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. No analytics, no tracking, no network requests are made by the extension.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at: github.com/dayoolacodes/audiofilter/issues
